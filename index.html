Hi, I’m Ashley.

I’m from Indianapolis and currently studying Finance and Sociology at the University of Notre Dame. A lot of what matters most to me lives outside of a resume — the people I love, the places I’ve gotten to explore, the energy of live music, and the routines and experiences that have shaped me over time.

I grew up running cross country and track, and that still shapes a lot of how I move through life. I care about discipline and consistency, but also about showing up well for the people around me. Some of my favorite memories have come from Notre Dame, traveling with friends, spending time outdoors, and saying yes to experiences that push me out of my comfort zone.

Professionally, I’m interested in investing, strategy, and middle-market businesses, but I wanted this site to feel a little more personal than that — more like a snapshot of what I enjoy, what I value, and the moments that have been meaningful to me.

A Few Things I Love

Running. Notre Dame football weekends. Traveling to new places. Coffee shops. Concerts with friends. Long conversations. Time with family. Being outside. The kind of memories that make a season of life feel distinct.
